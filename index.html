ABOUT US

Moli Group – a full-service digital media and tech company. Founded in 2019, with nearly 200 professional members of staff, we offer a wide range of digital and technological services. Originally headquartered in Vietnam, we have expanded our business to 6 countries worldwide.

Although we are a newly-formed company, our team have substantial experience in digital technology. Our ultimate goal is to present the next evolution of technology for the open internet.

With the motto: “Your Success is Our Success”, we strive to provide everyone with the feeling of a job well-done, both our clients and employees.
